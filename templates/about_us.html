Our Story

Growmate was created by a group of students passionate about solving the challenges farmers face with soil and plant health. We developed tools that provide instant support and promote learning in agriculture. By sharing knowledge and earning rewards, farmers can enhance their skills and make informed decisions. Mission

To equip farmers with simple, effective tools for assessing soil fertility and identifying plant diseases, while promoting a community focused on sustainable farming and environmental care.

Vision

To transform farming through technology, creating a future where every farmer has the knowledge and resources to cultivate healthy crops sustainably and protect the environment.
